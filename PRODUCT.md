# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers and AI engineers who maintain repositories with several agent instruction files, such as `AGENTS.md`, `CLAUDE.md`, Cursor rules, and Copilot instructions.

## Product Purpose

`agentchecker` finds contradictions between AI agent instruction files and helps resolve them before different tools follow conflicting rules.

## Brand Personality

Clinical, efficient, technically rigorous. The landing should feel like a precise diagnostic console for builders, with fast feedback and clear confidence.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, soft rounded marketing cards, beige editorial pages, decorative illustrations, and vague AI productivity copy.

## Design Principles

- Show the product as a working diagnostic tool.
- Keep the interface dense, legible, and command-line native.
- Use green as an active signal, not decoration.
- Prefer exact claims and concrete commands over broad marketing language.
- Preserve a dark, high-contrast developer environment.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast on dark backgrounds, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion compatibility for non-essential animation.
